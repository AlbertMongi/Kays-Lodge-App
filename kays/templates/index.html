About Us

Welcome to Kays Lodge


Make a tranquil escape to our Dar es Salaam lodge on the Peninsula. Authentic service where your comfort, security and accommodation cleanliness is a top priority.

Our lodge has been operating in the outskirts of the Peninsula since 2014 with over 500 international guests each year, we are proud in achieving guest satisfaction for comfort, security and cleanliness.

The lodge has 24 hrs Genset electricity back-up to run air conditioning units and 24 hrs reception service. The compound has electric fencing, CCTV, and protected by our own security guard in a gated compound sufficient for up to 8 vehicles.
